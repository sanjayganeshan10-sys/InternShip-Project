[GENERAL ILLNESS RECOVERY PROTOCOLS]
1. Common Cold & Upper Respiratory Infections:
   - Hydration: Consume at least 2 to 3 liters of fluids daily (water, clear broths, herbal teas).
   - Rest: Prioritize 8 to 9 hours of sleep to support immune function.
   - Recovery Timeline: Symptoms typically peak around days 3 to 5 and gradually resolve within 7 to 10 days.

2. Seasonal Influenza (Flu) Recovery:
   - Management: Focus on complete bed rest and fever management. 
   - Protocol: Avoid strenuous physical activity until at least 48 hours after a fever has naturally subsided without the use of fever-reducing medications.

3. Post-Viral Fatigue Care:
   - Energy Pacing: Gradually reintroduce low-impact physical activity (like light walking). Avoid high-intensity training during the immediate 2-week post-recovery window to prevent relapse.

[NUTRITIONAL SUPPORT FOR RECOVERY]
- Macronutrient Focus: Prioritize lean proteins (chicken, fish, tofu, lentils) to assist in cellular repair.
- Micronutrient Focus: Incorporate foods rich in Vitamin C (citrus fruits, bell peppers) and Zinc (pumpkin seeds, legumes) to optimize immune system recovery pathways.
